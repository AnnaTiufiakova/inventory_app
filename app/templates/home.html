Welcome to the Restaurant Inventory Management App

This application is built to help manage the inventory of a restaurant efficiently. It streamlines how ingredients and supplies are tracked, recorded, and reported.

The system is divided into three main sections:

  • Inventory: View and manage the list of all available items and their units. You can add new items or update existing ones.
  • Actions: Log daily activities like deliveries, sales, consumption, or waste. Each record supports optional photo uploads for delivery validation.
  • Inventory Setup: Add and organize inventory items, categories and measurement units for accurate tracking.
  • Reports: Visualize item usage trends over time. This helps track stock movement, optimize ordering, and reduce waste.

Use the navigation menu at the top to explore each section.
